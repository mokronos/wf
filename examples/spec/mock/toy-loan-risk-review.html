Toy Loan Risk Review

A fake finance workflow for testing scoring, policy explanations, manual exceptions, and decision auditability.

What It Should Do

Score a small loan application using deterministic arithmetic, approve low-risk cases, reject high-risk cases, and route borderline cases to a reviewer who can request more information or grant an exception.

Mocked Programs and Services

  • Applicant fixture JSON.
  • Pure scoring function with no credit bureau dependency.
  • Reviewer signal queue for exception decisions.
  • Generated decision letter as local text artifact.

Mermaid Diagram

flowchart TD
  A[Load application] --> B[Validate fields]
  B --> C{Missing data?}
  C -- yes --> D[Request more info]
  D --> E{Info received before deadline?}
  E -- no --> F[Close incomplete]
  E -- yes --> B
  C -- no --> G[Calculate toy risk score]
  G --> H{Decision band}
  H -- approve --> I[Generate approval letter]
  H -- reject --> J[Generate rejection letter]
  H -- review --> K[Open reviewer task]
  K --> L{Reviewer decision}
  L -- approve_exception --> I
  L -- reject --> J
  I --> M[Persist decision package]
  J --> M
