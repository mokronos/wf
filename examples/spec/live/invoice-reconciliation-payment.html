Invoice Reconciliation and Payment

Business workflow for accounts payable, three-way matching, exception handling, approvals, and payment execution.

What It Should Do

Ingest invoices from email and portals, extract invoice data, match against purchase orders and receiving records, route exceptions, schedule payment, and reconcile bank settlement without paying duplicates or unapproved invoices.

Programs and Services Involved

Inputs
AP mailbox, vendor portal downloads, scanned PDFs, EDI files, purchase order system.
Finance Systems
ERP, accounting system, payment processor, banking API, tax validation, currency rates.
Controls
Approval matrix, duplicate detector, fraud rules, audit archive, exception dashboard.

Workflow Diagram

flowchart TD
  A[Invoice arrives by email, portal, scan, or EDI] --> B[Classify vendor and document type]
  B --> C[Extract invoice fields and line items]
  C --> D{Duplicate invoice?}
  D -- yes --> E[Hold and notify AP analyst]
  D -- no --> F[Match to vendor, PO, and receipt]
  F --> G{Three-way match passed?}
  G -- yes --> H[Apply payment terms and discount rules]
  G -- no --> I[Route exception to buyer, receiver, or vendor]
  I --> J{Exception resolved?}
  J -- no --> K[Escalate before due date]
  K --> I
  J -- yes --> H
  H --> L{Approval required?}
  L -- yes --> M[Route by amount, department, and risk]
  L -- no --> N[Schedule payment batch]
  M --> N
  N --> O[Submit payment file or API request]
  O --> P[Reconcile settlement and update ERP]

Important Branches and Edge Cases

  • Invoice currency differs from the PO currency and needs rate locking plus approval of variance.
  • Partial receipt means some lines can be approved while others remain blocked.
  • Vendor changes bank details shortly before payment, requiring fraud review and payment hold.
  • Payment processor accepts a batch but rejects one payment, requiring item-level compensation.
  • Early payment discount expires soon, so unresolved exceptions must escalate faster.
  • Credit memo arrives after invoice approval and must net against the scheduled payment.
